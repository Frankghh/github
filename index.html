关于我们

About Us

本平台由龙岗区智能数字经济安全重点实验室（iDES）建立，由香港中文大学（深圳）数据科学学院吴保元教授领导。iDES致力于开发先进的可信AI技术和系统，涵盖对抗机器学习、隐私与版权保护、深度伪造检测、生成模型等领域，使AI更好地服务人类社会。如果您有兴趣合作，请联系我们 (wubaoyuan@cuhk.edu.cn)。

This platform is established by Longgang District Key Laboratory of Intelligent Digital Economy Security (iDES), led by Professor Baoyuan Wu from the School of Data Science, the Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen (CUHK-Shenzhen). The iDES aims to develop advanced trustworthy AI technologies and systems, covering adversarial machine learning, privacy and copyright protection, deepfake detection, generative models, to enable AI to better serve human society. If you are interested in cooperation, please contact us (wubaoyuan@cuhk.edu.cn).
