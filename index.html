About Us

This platform is established by Longgang District Key Laboratory of Intelligent Digital Economy Security (iDES), led by Professor Baoyuan Wu from the School of Data Science, the Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen (CUHK-Shenzhen). The iDES aims to develop advanced trustworthy AI technologies and systems, covering adversarial machine learning, privacy and copyright protection, deepfake detection, generative models, to enable AI to better serve human society.
